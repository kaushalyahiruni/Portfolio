App Designs

Cherish & Co. is a personalized gifting mobile application I developed as part of my university project, designed to simplify the way users discover, customize, and purchase meaningful gifts. The app features a curated product catalog, intuitive gift customization options, and a smooth, secure checkout process— creating a seamless end-to-end gifting experience that blends convenience, creativity, and user-friendly design.

I contributed to developing a desktop Bookshop POS System using Java, MySQL, and NetBeans, where our team built a user-friendly application that manages sales, billing, inventory, and product details. This project strengthened my skills in software development, database integration, and teamwork while giving me practical insight into real bookshop operations.
